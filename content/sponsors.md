# Empowering the Next Generation of Engineers Through Hands-On Innovation

TMU Battlebots is a student-led design team focused on innovation, healthy competition, hands-on experience, and collaboration through combat robotics. Combat robotics challenges students to troubleshoot and innovate under pressures such as BotBrawl - skills that are necessary for success in the real world.

By sponsoring TMU Battlebots, sponsors are given direct access to this diverse community of future engineers while increasing brand visibility within and beyond the TMU engineering community.


## 1. Access to emerging engineering talent

TMU Battlebots brings together engineering students from electrical, mechanical, mechatronics, and computer disciplines. Sponsors gain direct exposure to these engineering students who have already gained hands-on experience through design, manufacturing, electronic integration, or software development, allowing for more targeted and direct recruitment.


## 2. Opportunities for brand visibility

TMU Battlebots is a very active design team that participates in multiple events throughout the school year, making us a perfect partner for sponsors wanting more brand visibility and exposure:

Competitions: TMU Battlebots participates in BotBrawl Canada’s annual competition, giving sponsors a chance to showcase their brand to students and competitors across the country, as well as industry professionals.

Fairs/Showcases: Club fairs and showcases allow sponsors to be viewed by TMU students and faculty alike.

Website: Sponsor logos will be featured on the home page of the TMU Battlebots website, increasing visibility to a broader audience.

# As a student-led design team, we are always eager to forge partnerships with like-minded sponsors that share our passion for engineering, innovation, hands-on experience, and pushing the limits of competitive robotics.
